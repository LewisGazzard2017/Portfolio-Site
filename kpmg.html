KPMG - BRIGHT Day.

Alongside Oasis Academy Brightstowe.

The ‘BRIGHT Day’ event has been run by KPMG for the past three years and this year, law firm Bond Dickinson and catering company Nisbets also took part.

I was invited to produce a video about the day, as a thankyou from Oasis Academy Brightstowe. The video needed to incorporate students opinions, and show that the day is helpful for students, and that they do appreciate the time taken for KPMG to orgranise the event.

I had a very short timescale to edit the video, however it was completed on time, and the client was very happy with the final product
